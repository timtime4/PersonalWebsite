About Tim

Hola! My name is Tim Pusateri and welcome to my website! I'm currently a junior computer science major in Notre Dame's College of Engineering, and starting in the 2015 Spring semester I will start working toward obtaining a concentration in Cyber Security. I'm originally from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, but I call Sorin College my home at Notre Dame. Currently, I am studying abroad with Notre Dame's London program. In fact, as a tribute to my time in London, you might notice the font on this page matches that of the famous Johnson P22 typeface - the print used throughout the London Underground. After visiting ten different countries and meeting a ton of fantastic people, there's no doubt studying abroad has been one the best experiences of my life. In addition to learning all I can about computers and traveling, I love playing guitar, seeing great concerts, hiking, biking, and rooting for the Pittsburgh Penguins.
